About Rivyon EV

The Real Problem

Fuel prices keep rising every year. Daily commuting costs are painful for middle-income users.

Existing EVs are either expensive or over-engineered.

Licensing and paperwork stop many people from adopting EVs.

Mobility is no longer affordable for the average Indian household.

Our Approach

We are building practical electric scooters focused on everyday Indian users.

  • Easy charging at home
  • Built for daily use, not racing
  • No license or registration complexity
  • Simple and affordable EVs
